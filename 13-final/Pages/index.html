Reading Clubs

The reading clubs meet the second Saturday of each month from 9:00 a.m. to 12:00 noon at seven elementary schools near downtown Los Angeles. First-time volunteers begin the day by participating in a training session about reading aloud to children. While the new volunteers are being trained, returning volunteers participate in their own training program, focusing on improving the quality of our reading clubs.
