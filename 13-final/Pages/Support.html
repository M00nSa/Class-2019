Reading to Kids Depends on Generous Support of People Who Share the Same Vision as Us.

Change a Life Today

By making a gift today, You can help Reading to Kids maintain and expand the reading club program, purchase teacher-selected books for elementary school libraries in low-income communities, and fund book donations directly to children to aid them in developing their own home libraries.

Donate By Check

To donate by mailing us a check, please send your check to:

Reading to Kids, 1600 Sawtelle Blvd., Suite 210, Los Angeles, CA 90025.

Our Supporters

Reading to Kids would like to specially acknowledge various organizations which provide assistance to the reading club program by donating much-needed funds.

Donate By Grocery Store Card

Please register, at no cost to you, your grocery store club cards through Ralphs and eScrip so that participating retailers will contribute a portion of your purchases to Reading to Kids.
