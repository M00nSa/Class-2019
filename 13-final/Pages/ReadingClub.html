Organization and Goals

The reading clubs meet the second Saturday of each month from 9:00 a.m. to 12:00 noon at eight elementary schools near downtown Los Angeles.First-time volunteers participate in a training session about reading aloud to children. Returning volunteers review the books and develop topics of discussion and craft ideas. Children are signed in by teachers and administrators, and the fun begins at 10:00 a.m.

Reading Clubs

The children are divided into several small groups by grade level. First-time volunteers are paired with returning volunteers and assigned to each of these "reading club" groups. The volunteers read a pre-assigned book during a ninety-minute session, which also includes an arts and crafts project related to the book being read. At the end of the session, volunteers provide each child with a book of their own as a reward for attending.
