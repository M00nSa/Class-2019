About

Coffee Tomo

Tomo is the Japanese word for friend. We incorporated the notion of friendship into our logo by using two coffee cups to represent companionship, as cups of coffee are often shared among friends. Coffee Tomo offers high-quality products in an upscale environment.

Our Specialties

In-house coffee roasting

Our coffee and pretzels are not “fast food.” We personally roast our beans and prepare a custom cup of coffee for our guests.

Fresh daily pretzels

Our pretzel dough and toast are made daily in-house from scratch to ensure premium freshness for our customers.
