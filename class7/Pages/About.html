Coffee Tomo

Tomo is the Japanese word for friend. We incorporated the notion of friendship into our logo by using two coffee cups to represent companionship, as cups of coffee are often shared among friends. Coffee Tomo offers high-quality products in an upscale environment.

Our Specialties

Our coffee and pretzels are not “fast food.” We personally roast our beans and prepare a custom cup of coffee for our guests.

Fresh daily pretzels

Our pretzel dough and toast are made daily in-house from scratch to ensure premium freshness for our customers.

Hand Drip Coffee

HAND DRIP MEANS MAKING COFFEE USING KETTLE AND HOT WATER BY HAND. TO MAKE GOOD COFFEE, IT IS IMPORTANT TO HAVE AN EXCELLENT COMMAND OF SUCH A DIFFICULT TECHNIQUE. HAND DRIP HAS ALWAYS BEEN OF THE MOST POPULAR METHODS OF MAKING COFFEE IN ASIAN COUNTRIES LIKE KOREA AND JAPAN. HOWEVER, IT IS INTRODUCED TO AMERICA A FEW YEARS AGO.

ONLY THE BEST BEANS ARE HAND-SELECTED AND ROASTED AT COFFEE TOMO. WE OFFER CUSTOMERS THE OPPORTUNITY TO SELECT THEIR BEANS AND PERSONALLY GROUND THEM. WE USE VARIOUS TYPES OF DRIPPERS SUCH AS KALITA, KONO, AND HARIO. WE PERFECTED OUR HAND DRIP SKILLS TO CREATE THE BEST COFFEE FOR OUR CUSTOMERS. HAND DRIP COFFEE IS DIFFERENT FROM MACHINE BREW DRIP COFFEE. OUR SKILLED BARISTAS CAN CONTROL THE TASTE OF THE COFFEE AS WELL AS CUSTOMIZE THE ORDER TO MEET EVERY CUSTOMER’S TASTES.
