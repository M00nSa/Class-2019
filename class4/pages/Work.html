OBABY BITE STOOL

Designed for XO

Baby Bite is a zoomorphic object. It is a stool, a side table or a table base, all in one. Like a children's game, they fit into each other and thus create new shapes.

Self-Warming Bottle

Iiamo Baby Bottle

Iiamo go is the first baby feeding bottle that warms milk up to the recommended 37°C (body temperature) in about 4 minutes, without the use of electricity.

Wall-Mounted mirror

Oskar

A rounded wall mirror, Oskar’s frame is in rotomoulded polyethylene. Besides its use as a mirror, its front pocket-compartment with two hooks can be used for storing and hanging numerous everyday objects.

Doux Lounge Chair

Designed for Vondom

Doux, a collection of different pieces derives directly from freehand sketches: organic, spontaneous, friendly, and colourful. The zoomorphic forms are to engage, to touch, to sit, to enjoy.

Vertex set

Designed for Vondom

The collection VERTEX is created for integrating into our space a more conceptual architectural piece, to give it a more sophisticated touch. The pieces are manufactured by rotational molding, the main characteristic of this technique is that the material is 100% recyclable and it can explore never-ending possibilities in the shapes.

LAVA Bench

Designed for Vondom

LAVA is an undulating, cloud-like bench, and an organic, rounded sculpture. It is an original and different way for the user and furniture to interact.

V-SURF Tumbona

V-SURF

The flowing wave that composes the seat and leg rest of SURF alludes to a sense of calm. It is a soft swell in lux, a fluid gesture that changes as the light does.
